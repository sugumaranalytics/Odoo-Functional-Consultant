# Question 1

## What is an Opportunity?

### Answer

An Opportunity is a qualified Lead that has a high probability of becoming a customer.

---

# Question 2

## Why is Opportunity Management important?

### Answer

Opportunity Management helps businesses:

- Track qualified customers
- Improve follow-ups
- Increase sales conversion
- Forecast future revenue
- Manage the sales pipeline efficiently

---

# Question 3

## What information is stored in an Opportunity?

### Answer

An Opportunity stores:

- Customer Name
- Opportunity Name
- Salesperson
- Email
- Phone
- Expected Revenue
- Probability
- Expected Closing Date
- Priority
- Tags
- Activities
- Internal Notes

---

# Question 4

## Explain the Opportunity Lifecycle.

### Answer

```text
Lead
   ↓
Opportunity
   ↓
Qualified
   ↓
Proposition
   ↓
Won
```

If the customer rejects the proposal:

```text
Opportunity
      ↓
Lost
```

---

# Question 5

## Explain the CRM Pipeline stages.

### Answer

| Stage | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| New | Customer inquiry received |
| Qualified | Customer is interested and requirements are confirmed |
| Proposition | Proposal or quotation sent |
| Won | Customer accepted the proposal |
| Lost | Customer rejected the proposal |

---

# Question 6

## What is Expected Revenue?

### Answer

Expected Revenue is the estimated sales value expected from an Opportunity.

**Example**

₹50,000

---

# Question 7

## What is Probability?

### Answer

Probability indicates the chance of winning an Opportunity.

Example:

- 20% – Low chance
- 50% – Moderate chance
- 80% – High chance
- 100% – Opportunity Won

---

# Question 8

## Why are Activities used?

### Answer

Activities help salespeople remember follow-up tasks such as:

- Phone Calls
- Meetings
- Emails
- Product Demonstrations
- To-Do Tasks

---

# Question 9

## What are Internal Notes?

### Answer

Internal Notes are private notes visible only to company employees.

Example

```text
Customer requested a product demo next week.
```

---

# Question 10

## Why are Tags used?

### Answer

Tags organize Opportunities into categories for easier searching and reporting.

Examples

- ERP
- Odoo
- Retail
- Manufacturing
- High Value
