# What is an Opportunity?

An **Opportunity** is a qualified Lead that has a high chance of becoming a customer.

Once a Lead is qualified, it is converted into an Opportunity.

Opportunities are managed until they become either:

- Won
- Lost

---

# Lead vs Opportunity

| Lead | Opportunity |
|------|-------------|
| Initial customer inquiry | Qualified customer |
| Budget may not be known | Budget is confirmed |
| Purchase not confirmed | High chance of purchase |
| Early sales stage | Ready for quotation |

# Opportunity Stages

## New

Customer inquiry received.

Example

Customer contacted sales team.

---

## Qualified

Customer is interested.

Requirements understood.

Budget confirmed.

---

## Proposition

Quotation has been prepared or sent.

Customer is reviewing the proposal.

---

## Won

Customer accepted the quotation.

Sales Order can be created.

---

## Lost

Customer rejected the quotation.

Possible reasons

- Budget issue
- Competitor selected
- Requirement cancelled

# Probability

Probability indicates the chance of winning the Opportunity.

Examples

| Probability | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 10% | Low chance |
| 40% | Moderate chance |
| 70% | High chance |
| 100% | Won Opportunity |
