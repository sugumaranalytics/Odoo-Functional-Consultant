# What is the Sales Module?

The **Sales Module** in Odoo helps businesses manage the complete sales process from preparing a quotation to receiving customer payment.

It helps companies:

- Create Quotations
- Confirm Sales Orders
- Manage Customers
- Sell Products and Services
- Create Invoices
- Track Sales Performance

---

# Why is the Sales Module Important?

Without the Sales Module

- Manual quotations
- Difficult order tracking
- Paper-based sales process
- Delayed invoicing
- Poor customer service

With the Sales Module

- Faster quotations
- Easy Sales Order management
- Better customer experience
- Accurate pricing
- Integrated invoicing
- Better reporting

# Interview Questions

## What is the Sales Module?

The Sales Module manages quotations, sales orders, invoicing, and customer sales.

---

## What is a Quotation?

A quotation is a price offer sent to the customer before the sale is confirmed.

---

## What is a Sales Order?

A Sales Order is created after the customer accepts the quotation.

---

## What is an Invoice?

An Invoice is a bill requesting payment from the customer.

---

## What happens after Payment?

The Invoice status changes to **Paid**.

---

## Why are Payment Terms important?

Payment Terms define when the customer should make payment.

---

## What is the purpose of Chatter?

Chatter records messages, notes, activities, and communication related to sales documents.

---

## What is the difference between a Quotation and a Sales Order?

Quotation is an offer.

Sales Order is the customer's confirmed purchase.

---

## What is the purpose of Terms and Conditions?

Terms and Conditions define the business rules for the sale.

---

## Why do businesses use the Sales Module?

Businesses use it to manage the complete sales process efficiently from quotation to payment.
