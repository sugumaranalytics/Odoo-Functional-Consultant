# Objective

Practice creating, managing, qualifying, and converting Leads into Opportunities using the Odoo CRM module.

# Question 1

## What is a Lead?

### Answer

A Lead is a potential customer who has shown interest in a company's product or service but has not yet been qualified by the sales team.

---

# Question 2

## What is the purpose of Lead Management?

### Answer

Lead Management helps businesses:

- Capture customer inquiries
- Organize customer information
- Schedule follow-ups
- Improve sales conversions
- Convert Leads into Opportunities

---

# Question 6

## What is Lead Qualification?

### Answer

Lead Qualification is the process of checking whether a customer has genuine interest, budget, and the ability to purchase a product or service.

---

# Question 7

## What is the difference between a Lead and an Opportunity?

### Answer

| Lead | Opportunity |
|------|-------------|
| Potential customer | Qualified customer |
| Initial inquiry | Customer is interested in buying |
| Budget may not be known | Budget is usually confirmed |
| Not ready for quotation | Ready for quotation |

---

# Question 8

## Why are Activities important?

### Answer

Activities remind salespeople to perform follow-ups such as:

- Phone Calls
- Meetings
- Emails
- Product Demonstrations

They help ensure no customer inquiry is forgotten.

---

# Question 9

## What are Internal Notes?

### Answer

Internal Notes are private notes visible only to company employees.

Example:

```text
Customer wants cloud-based ERP.
Decision maker is Finance Manager.
```

---

# Question 10

## Why are Tags used?

### Answer

Tags help organize and categorize Leads.

Examples:

- ERP
- Manufacturing
- Retail
- Hospital
- Education

# Interview Questions

## What is a Lead?

**Answer**

A Lead is a potential customer interested in a company's product or service.

---

## What is Lead Qualification?

**Answer**

It is the process of checking customer interest, budget, and purchase potential.

---

## What is Lead Conversion?

**Answer**

Converting a qualified Lead into an Opportunity.

---

## Why do companies use Lead Management?

**Answer**

To organize customer inquiries, improve follow-ups, and increase sales conversions.

---

## What is a Lead Source?

**Answer**

It identifies where the customer inquiry came from.

Examples:

- Website
- Facebook
- Google Ads
- Referral

---

## Why are Activities important?

**Answer**

Activities remind salespeople about calls, meetings, emails, and follow-ups.

---

## What are Internal Notes?

**Answer**

Private notes used by employees for recording important customer information.

---

## What are Tags?

**Answer**

Tags help organize and filter Leads.

Examples:

- ERP
- Retail
- Manufacturing
- Odoo

---

## What is Lead Assignment?

**Answer**

Assigning a Lead to a salesperson who is responsible for managing it.

---

## What is a Lost Lead?

**Answer**

A Lead that did not convert into an Opportunity because the customer was not interested, had no budget, or selected another company.
