# What is a Lead?

A **Lead** is a person or company that has shown interest in your product or service but has not yet been qualified by the sales team.

A Lead is the first stage of the sales process.

Examples

- Customer fills a website contact form.
- Customer sends an email asking for product details.
- Customer calls the company.
- Customer visits a trade exhibition.
- Customer contacts through social media.

# Why Companies Use Leads

Without Lead Management

- Customer details may be forgotten.
- Follow-ups may be missed.
- Sales opportunities may be lost.
- Duplicate customer records may occur.

With Lead Management

- Every inquiry is recorded.
- Salespeople receive assigned leads.
- Follow-ups are scheduled.
- Conversion rates improve.
- Reports become accurate.
