# Objective

Learn how to create professional quotations, manage quotation details, send quotations to customers, and convert quotations into Sales Orders in Odoo.

---

# Question 1

## What is a Quotation?

### Answer

A Quotation is a formal price offer sent to a customer before confirming a sale. It includes product details, quantity, pricing, taxes, payment terms, and conditions.

---

# Question 2

## Why is a Quotation important?

### Answer

A quotation helps to:

- Inform customers about prices.
- Explain products or services.
- Define payment and delivery terms.
- Avoid misunderstandings.
- Obtain customer approval before confirming the sale.

---

# Question 3

## What information is included in a Quotation?

### Answer

A quotation usually contains:

- Customer Name
- Quotation Date
- Product Details
- Quantity
- Unit Price
- Taxes
- Total Amount
- Payment Terms
- Terms and Conditions

---

---

# Question 5

## What is the difference between a Quotation and a Sales Order?

### Answer

| Quotation | Sales Order |
|-----------|-------------|
| Price offer | Confirmed customer order |
| Customer has not approved | Customer has approved |
| Can be modified | Official sales document |
| Before sale | After customer confirmation |

---

# Question 6

## What are Terms and Conditions?

### Answer

Terms and Conditions define the rules of the sale such as payment, delivery, warranty, and installation.

Example:

- Delivery within 7 working days.
- One-year warranty.
- Payment before delivery.

---

# Question 7

## What are Optional Products?

### Answer

Optional Products are additional items suggested to the customer to increase sales.

Examples:

- Mouse
- Keyboard
- Printer
- Laptop Bag

---

# Question 8

## What is the purpose of Internal Notes?

### Answer

Internal Notes store private information for company employees.

Example:

```text
Customer requested delivery on Friday.
```

---

# Question 9

## Why are Activities used?

### Answer

Activities remind salespeople about follow-up tasks such as:

- Call Customer
- Send Email
- Arrange Meeting
- Product Demo

---

# Question 10

## What happens after the customer approves a quotation?

### Answer

The quotation is confirmed and converted into a Sales Order.
