# What is the Contacts Module?

The **Contacts Module** is the central place for managing all business contacts in Odoo.

It stores information about:

- Customers
- Vendors
- Companies
- Individual Contacts

The same contact information is shared across Sales, Purchase, CRM, Inventory, Accounting, and other Odoo modules.

---

# Why is the Contacts Module Important?

Every business deals with customers and vendors.

Instead of entering contact details repeatedly, Odoo stores them once in the Contacts module.

Benefits:

- Centralized Contact Management
- No Duplicate Data
- Easy Search
- Better Communication
- Shared Across All Modules

---

# Types of Contacts

Odoo supports two types of contacts.

### Company

Represents a business or organization.

Examples:

- ABC Technologies
- XYZ Suppliers
- Ravinder Technologies Pvt Ltd

---

### Individual

Represents a person.

Examples:

- Raj Kumar
- John Sale
- Sales Manager

Individuals can belong to a company.

# Creating a Customer

Navigation:

Contacts

↓

New

Enter:

- Company Name
- Address
- Phone
- Mobile
- Email
- Website

Click **Save**.

The customer can now be used in Sales and CRM.

---

# Creating a Vendor

Navigation:

Contacts

↓

New

↓

Company

Enter:

- Vendor Name
- Address
- Phone
- Email
- Website

Click **Save**.

The vendor can now be selected while creating Purchase Requests for Quotation (RFQs).

# Contact Information

Each contact record can contain:

- Company Name
- Contact Person
- Address

- State
- Country
- Phone
- Mobile
- Email
- Website
- GSTIN
- PAN
- Tags
- Internal Notes

---

# Parent & Child Contacts

A company can have multiple contact persons.

Example:

XYZ Suppliers

↓

Raj Kumar

↓

Purchase Manager

Benefits:

- Better communication
- Department-wise contacts
- Organized customer information

---

# Contacts & Addresses

A company may have multiple addresses.

Examples:

- Office Address
- Billing Address
- Shipping Address
- Branch Office

Each address can be managed separately.

---

# Sales & Purchase Information

The **Sales & Purchase** tab contains information such as:

- Salesperson
- Sales Team
- Payment Terms
- Pricelist
- Delivery Method
- Purchase Information

These settings are used automatically while creating quotations and purchase orders.

---

# Invoicing Information

The **Invoicing** tab contains:

- GST Information
- Tax Details
- Bank Accounts
- Fiscal Position
- Payment Configuration

This information is used while creating invoices.

---

# Search Contacts

Use the **Search Bar** to quickly find contacts.

Example:

Search:

XYZ Suppliers

Result:

Vendor Record

Searching saves time and improves productivity.

---

# Filters

Filters display only the required contacts.

Examples:

- Companies
- Individuals
- Customers
- Vendors

Filters help users manage large numbers of contacts efficiently.

---

# Contact Tags

Tags help categorize contacts.

Examples:

- VIP Customer
- Supplier
- Distributor
- Premium Customer
- Retail Customer

Tags make searching and reporting easier.

---

# Chatter

Every contact contains a **Chatter** section.

The Chatter records:

- Contact Created
- Address Updated
- Email Changed
- Phone Updated
- Notes
- Activities

It provides a complete history of changes.

---

# Integration with Other Modules

The Contacts module is connected to:

- CRM
- Sales
- Purchase
- Inventory
- Accounting
- Helpdesk
- Projects

A contact created once can be used across all business processes.

# Best Practices

- Create company records before adding contact persons.
- Keep contact information updated.
- Use meaningful tags.
- Maintain separate billing and shipping addresses.
- Avoid duplicate contact records.
- Verify email and phone numbers regularly.
