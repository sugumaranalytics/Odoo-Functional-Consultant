As an Odoo Functional Consultant

One of your most common client conversations may be:

Client: "We already use Tally. Why should we move to Odoo?"

Your answer should be:

"Tally is an excellent accounting software and many businesses continue to use it successfully. 
If your company mainly needs accounting and GST compliance, Tally may be sufficient. However, 
if you want to integrate Sales, CRM, Purchase, Inventory, HR, Manufacturing, Projects, and Accounting into a single system
with real-time data, Odoo provides a more comprehensive ERP solution."

This balanced explanation is more professional than saying one product is "better" than the other, 
because the right choice depends on the company's size, processes, and business goals.

Why Tally is so popular in India?

For many years, businesses have used TallyPrime (previously Tally.ERP 9) because it is excellent for:

Accounting
GST filing
Invoicing
Payroll
Financial statements
Tax compliance

That's why accountants and finance departments are very familiar with Tally, especially in small and medium-sized businesses.

Is Tally a complete ERP?

Partially.

Tally includes accounting and some inventory features, but its primary strength is finance and compliance rather than 
managing every business department. As businesses grow, they often need additional systems for CRM, HR, manufacturing, 
customer support, projects, or eCommerce.
