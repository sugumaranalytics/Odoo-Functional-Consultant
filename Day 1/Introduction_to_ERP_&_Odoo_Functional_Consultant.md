=========

Typical responsibilities in a remote Functional Consultant role
Attend client meetings on Zoom or Google Meet.
Understand the client's business processes.
Configure Odoo modules.
Train users online.
Test the system before deployment.
Prepare documentation (BRD, FRD, UAT, user manuals).
Coordinate with technical developers.

Most of this work can be done from home.
