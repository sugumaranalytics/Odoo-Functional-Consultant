# Assignment – Day 2

## Scenario

You are an Odoo Functional Consultant.

A new employee joins the Sales Department.

Answer the following questions.

---

# 1. Which module should the employee open?

**Answer:**

The employee should open the **Sales** module.

The Sales module is used to manage:

- Quotations
- Sales Orders
- Customers
- Products
- Sales Reports

It is the primary module used by the Sales Department for daily business operations.

---

# 2. How can the employee search for a customer?

**Answer:**

The employee can search for a customer using the **Search Bar** at the top of the screen.

**Steps:**

1. Open the Sales or Contacts module.
2. Click the Search Bar.
3. Enter the customer name.
4. Press **Enter**.
5. Select the required customer record.

This allows users to quickly find customer information without browsing through all records.

---

# 3. What is the purpose of Filters?

**Answer:**

Filters are used to display only the records that match specific conditions.

Examples:

- My Quotations
- Draft Quotations
- Confirmed Sales Orders
- Today's Activities

**Benefits:**

- Saves time
- Quickly finds required records
- Improves productivity
- Makes data analysis easier

---

# 4. Why is Kanban View useful?

**Answer:**

Kanban View displays records as visual cards instead of rows.

It helps users:

- Track the status of work
- Move records between stages
- Understand progress at a glance

For example, in the CRM module, opportunities can move through stages such as:

Lead

↓

Qualified

↓

Proposal

↓

Won

This visual workflow helps the Sales team monitor customer opportunities efficiently.

---

# 5. What are Activities used for?

**Answer:**

Activities help users remember and manage important tasks.

Examples include:

- Customer follow-up calls
- Meetings
- Emails
- Product demonstrations
- Task reminders
- Deadlines

Activities improve organization and ensure important work is completed on time.

---

# 6. Why is Multi-Company support important?

**Answer:**

Multi-Company support allows a single Odoo system to manage multiple companies while keeping each company's data separate.

**Example:**

A business owner has:

- ABC Supermarket
- XYZ Textile Store

Both companies can be managed from one Odoo database.

**Benefits:**

- Easy company switching
- Separate accounting records
- Separate inventory
- Centralized management
- Reduced software costs

---

# 7. What user settings can be configured?

**Answer:**

Each user can configure personal settings, including:

- Language
- Time Zone
- Password
- Email Signature
- Profile Information
- Notifications
- Preferences

These settings personalize the Odoo experience and improve usability.

---

# Conclusion

As an Odoo Functional Consultant, it is important to train new employees on navigating the Odoo interface, 
searching for records, using filters and views, managing activities, and configuring personal settings. 
A good understanding of these features helps users work efficiently and increases productivity.
