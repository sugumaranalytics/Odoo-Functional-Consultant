# Interview Questions – Day 2

## 1. What is the Apps Dashboard?

**Answer:**

The Apps Dashboard is the main screen in Odoo that displays all installed business applications (modules).

Examples:

- CRM
- Sales
- Purchase
- Inventory
- Accounting
- HR
- Manufacturing

It allows users to quickly access different business modules.

---

## 2. What is the purpose of the App Switcher?

**Answer:**

The App Switcher allows users to quickly move between different Odoo modules without logging out.

Example:

CRM

↓

Sales

↓

Inventory

↓

Accounting

This improves productivity and saves time.

---

## 3. Explain Menu and Submenu.

**Answer:**

Menus organize business functions within a module.

A submenu is a category under the main menu.

Example:

Sales

↓

Orders

↓

Quotations

↓

Sales Orders

Menus help users navigate the system efficiently.

---

## 4. What is the Search Bar used for?

**Answer:**

The Search Bar is used to quickly find records in Odoo.

Examples:

- Customer
- Product
- Sales Order
- Vendor
- Employee

It helps users locate information without manually browsing records.

---

## 5. What are Filters?

**Answer:**

Filters display only records that meet specific conditions.

Examples:

- My Quotations
- Draft Orders
- Confirmed Orders
- Today's Activities

Benefits:

- Saves time
- Improves productivity
- Makes searching easier

---

## 6. What is Group By?

**Answer:**

Group By organizes records into categories.

Example:

Sales Orders

↓

Group By

↓

Salesperson

Result:

Sales orders are grouped by each salesperson.

Other examples:

- Customer
- Product
- Month
- Status

---

## 7. What are Favorites?

**Answer:**

Favorites allow users to save frequently used searches and filters.

Example:

"My Customers"

Instead of creating the same filter every day, users can save it as a Favorite and access it with one click.

---

## 8. Explain List View.

**Answer:**

List View displays records in a table format.

Example:

| Customer | Order | Amount |
|-----------|-------|---------|
| ABC Ltd | SO001 | ₹15,000 |
| XYZ Ltd | SO002 | ₹8,500 |

It is useful for viewing many records quickly.

---

## 9. Explain Form View.

**Answer:**

Form View displays complete information about a single record.

Example:

Customer Details

- Name
- Address
- Phone
- Email
- GST Number

Users can create, edit, or update records in Form View.

---

## 10. Explain Kanban View.

**Answer:**

Kanban View displays records as visual cards.

Example:

Lead

↓

Qualified

↓

Proposal

↓

Won

Users can drag and drop cards between stages, making it ideal for CRM, Projects, and Helpdesk.

---

## 11. What is Calendar View?

**Answer:**

Calendar View displays records based on dates.

Examples:

- Meetings
- Appointments
- Interviews
- Follow-ups

It helps users plan and manage schedules.

---

## 12. What is Activity View?

**Answer:**

Activity View shows pending work and reminders.

Examples:

- Call Customer
- Send Email
- Schedule Meeting
- Prepare Quotation

It helps users complete tasks on time.

---

## 13. What is Pivot View?

**Answer:**

Pivot View is used for business analysis.

It summarizes data in a table format.

Examples:

- Total Sales
- Monthly Revenue
- Product-wise Sales
- Salesperson Performance

Managers use Pivot View for decision-making.

---

## 14. What is Graph View?

**Answer:**

Graph View displays business data using charts.

Examples:

- Bar Chart
- Line Chart
- Pie Chart

It helps management analyze business performance visually.

---

## 15. What user preferences can be configured?

**Answer:**

Users can configure:

- Language
- Time Zone
- Password
- Email Signature
- Notifications
- Profile Information
- Preferences

These settings personalize the Odoo experience.

---

## 16. What is Multi-Company?

**Answer:**

Multi-Company allows one Odoo system to manage multiple companies while keeping their data separate.

Example:

- ABC Supermarket
- XYZ Textiles

Benefits:

- Separate accounting
- Separate inventory
- Easy company switching
- Centralized management

---

## 17. What are Activities?

**Answer:**

Activities are reminders for business tasks.

Examples:

- Phone Calls
- Meetings
- Emails
- Follow-ups
- Deadlines

Activities help users stay organized and improve productivity.

---

## 18. What are Notifications?

**Answer:**

Notifications inform users about important updates.

Examples:

- New Messages
- Approval Requests
- Assigned Tasks
- Meeting Reminders
- System Alerts

Notifications keep users informed about ongoing work.

---

## 19. Why is Odoo easy to use?

**Answer:**

Odoo is easy to use because it offers:

- User-friendly interface
- Simple navigation
- Integrated modules
- Powerful search
- Multiple views
- Real-time information
- Easy customization
- Modern dashboard

These features make business management simple and efficient.

---

## 20. What is the responsibility of a Functional Consultant during user training?

**Answer:**

During user training, an Odoo Functional Consultant is responsible for:

- Explaining the Odoo interface
- Demonstrating business workflows
- Teaching users how to use modules
- Answering user questions
- Providing user manuals
- Conducting hands-on training
- Helping users become comfortable with the system
- Ensuring users can perform daily tasks independently

The goal of training is to ensure successful adoption of Odoo within the organization.

---

# Interview Tip

Remember these key points:

- **Apps Dashboard** → Displays all installed Odoo modules.
- **App Switcher** → Quickly move between modules.
- **Search Bar** → Find records instantly.
- **Filters** → Show only required records.
- **Group By** → Organize records into categories.
- **Favorites** → Save frequently used searches.
- **Kanban View** → Visual card-based workflow.
- **List View** → Table format.
- **Form View** → Detailed record information.
- **Calendar View** → Date-based scheduling.
- **Pivot View** → Data analysis.
- **Graph View** → Visual reports.
- **Activities** → Task reminders.
- **Notifications** → Business alerts.
- **Multi-Company** → Manage multiple companies in one Odoo database.
- **Functional Consultant** → Understand business, configure Odoo, train users, perform testing, and support ERP implementation.
